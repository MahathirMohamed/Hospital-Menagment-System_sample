Leptospirosis

Leptospirosis is a bacterial infection transmitted through contact with contaminated water or soil, often from infected animal urine. Symptoms vary from mild flu-like illness to severe complications. Prevention includes protective measures and vaccination for at-risk individuals. Early diagnosis and antibiotic treatment are vital for effective management

Interventional Radiology

Interventional radiology (IR) is a medical specialty using minimally invasive procedures guided by imaging. It includes various interventions like angiography and biopsies, providing less invasive alternatives to traditional surgery. IR procedures often lead to shorter recovery times and fewer complications, playing a crucial role in comprehensive patient care
